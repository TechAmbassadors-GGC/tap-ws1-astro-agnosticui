# Our First NSF-funded Teachers' Workshop

We had our first National Science Foundation (NFS) funded Teachers' Workshop on 
May 10th, 2024. We had 17 participants from the Gwinnett County School System
 (GCPS) and other educational nonprofits such as Next Generation Focus (NGF).
